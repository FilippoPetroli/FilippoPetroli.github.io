Day 0 - the beginning sucks

Sunday - June 28, 2026

the beginning sucks. for everything. simple as it is.

we're always waiting the best moment to do something meaningful.
On Monday I'll start the gym...
This weekend I'll have time to start this book...
Tomorrow I'll start eating healthy...
bullshit!
be honest with yourself, it doesn't work.

And it's not about lack of motivation - I'm aware that you really want to be healthy, start reading books and embrace any other significant change in your life but you don't start. I know this feeling, I've been postponing many projects in my life for a while too. And now I decided. Not only decided but started. Being honest, I should have started 3 days ago but I didn't. I knew I would have to fix many technical details on this website to post my thoughts and argh, "tomorrow I'll do it".. Well, it's Sunday night and I spent the whole weekend thinking about it, not acting. Tonight I decided to act.

I expected that on my day 0 I would be sharing a plan, goals,... well, not yet. Tomorrow. And no, I'm not postponing (sounds contradictory, I know) but I needed to spend some hours resolving the technical part - and that's part of the journey. Happy that at least I finished my day with everything ready to start sharing every day.

see you tomorrow.
